Grandma's lemons meringue pie

30 mins-prep time

10 Mins-cook time

40 Min-Total

servings

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2tablespoon all purpose flour
  • 3tbsp cornstarch
  • 1/4 tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 2 lemons,juiced ans zested
  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 4 eggs yolks,beaten
  • 1(9 inch)pie crust,baked
  • 6 tablespoon white sugar

DIRECTIONS

  1. preheat oven :preheat oven to 350degrees F(175degrees)
  2. make lemon filling:in a saucepan...
    • whisk together 1 cup sugar,flour,cornstarch,and salt
    • stir in water,lemon juice and lemon zest
    • cook over medium-high heat,stirring frequently until mixture comes to a boil.
    • stir in butter
    • place egge yolks in a small bowl and gradually whisk in 1/2 cup of hot sugar mixture.
    • whisk egg yolk mixture back into remaining sugar mixture
    • bring a boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly untill thick.
    • remove from heat
    • pour filling into baked pastry shell.
  3. Make meringue:in a large glas or metal bowl...
    • whip eggs until foamy
    • add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until sfiff peaks form
    • spread meringue over pie ,sealing the edges at the crust
  4. Bake:in preheated oven for 10 minutes,or until meringue is golden brown
